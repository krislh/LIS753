Desserts and Baking

Starchies

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup (120ml) sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/2 cup (115g) butter, softened
  • 2 cups (256g) cornstarch
  • 1 tsp almond extract

Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350F (165C).

2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the sweetened condensed milk, almond extract, and the butter. Beat smooth.

3. Using the handle of a wooden spoon, stir in the cornstarch two tablespoons at a time. Mix until a smooth dough is formed.

4. Divide by rounded teaspoons or small cookie scoop, lightly rolling in hands to form a smooth 2-inch ball. Place on a parchment-lined baking sheet.

5. Bake for 17-20 minutes. Cookies should not brown. Let cool briefly on the baking sheet bevore transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.

Apricot-Walnut Bars

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mashed ripe bananas (2 to 3 medium bananas
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/3 cup chopped dried apricots
  • 1/3 cup chopped walnuts
  • 2 cups old-fashioned rolled oats

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350F. Line an 8-by-8-inch dish with parchment paper.

In a medium bowl, combine the bananas, vanilla, brown sugar, and salt and stir to evenly combine.

Stir in the apricots and walnuts into the banana mixture until combined. Finally, stir in the oats.

Press the mixture into the botton of the prepared dish.

Bake for 30 minutes, or until edges are golden brown. Remove from baking dish and set on wire rack to cool, then cut into equal bars.
